Core Capabilities

Built for real care,
not just monitoring

AAROGYA fuses sensor data, AI inference, and closed-loop interventions — all at home, in real time.

🧠
AI Anomaly Detection
Personalised Z-score baselines detect deviations specific to your physiology — not generic population thresholds. Context-aware analysis reduces false positives.
🌬️
Closed-Loop Interventions
When SpO₂ dips, AAROGYA guides you through a breathing protocol and re-checks before escalating to a caregiver alert — avoiding unnecessary panic.
⚡
Fall Detection
MPU6050 accelerometer classifies sudden acceleration spikes and post-fall stillness, triggering instant WhatsApp alerts with location context.
🌡️
Multi-Sensor Fusion
Heart rate, SpO₂, temperature, air quality, humidity and motion — fused into a single live health score updated every second.
📊
Personalized Baseline
The system calibrates to your unique physiology over the first session, establishing a personal normal before anomaly detection activates.
🏠
Environment Monitoring
Room temperature, humidity, and AQI are tracked alongside vitals — correlating environmental factors with health changes over time.
Process

From sensor to safety

01
🔌
Sensors Collect
Wearable and environmental sensors continuously stream raw biometric data every second.
02
🧮
AI Analyses
Z-score engine compares readings against your personal baseline, flagging deviations in real time.
03
🎯

04

If vitals don't recover, WhatsApp/SMS alerts are sent instantly to designated caregivers.
